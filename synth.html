"Dataset Augmentation in Papyrology with Generative Models:A Study of Synthetic Ancient Greek Character Images"

IJCAI 2022 Supplemental Material

Thank you for your interest in our research!

In the spirit of open sourced research and experiment reproducibility, we offer supplemental material for our IJCAI 2022 submission. In the links below we offer sample synthetic character images produced during the project, a zipped code repository that demonstrates the utilization of Pytorch's 'simple' implementation of StyleGAN2, and the saved classification models with an inference demonstration Jupyter Notebook.

StyleGAN2 Pytorch Utilization Details

The generative code repository includes a bash script, setup.sh, that installs a Python 3.7 virtual environment with the necessary libraries, and a bash script, generate.sh, that demonstrates the command-line usage from our IJCAI paper. More information on the StyleGAN2 implementatoin is available here. Information on Python virtual environments can be found here.
